FOSS Club

Welcome to the Free and Open Source Software Club [FOSS] at PESCE! We are a vibrant community of students passionate about open-source technology. Our mission is to empower individuals through collaboration and innovation, fostering a spirit of inclusivity and learning. The PESCE FOSS CLUB is your gateway to discovering and contributing to real-world projects and making meaningful connections with industry experts.
